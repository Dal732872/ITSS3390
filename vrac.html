VRAC strengthens its leadership

Published: Spanish Rugby News — October 2025

VRAC Quesos Entrepinares continues to dominate the División de Honor B after a crucial victory in Sant Boi against UE Santboiana.

The Valladolid giants demonstrated once again why they are the benchmark of Spanish rugby, combining a powerful scrum, fast transitions, and exceptional kicking strategy to secure another win.

With this result, VRAC consolidates its position at the top of the table and extends the gap with its closest rivals, sending a strong message for the rest of the season.

The team has shown remarkable consistency and mental strength, especially in high-pressure matches away from home. Their ability to control the tempo and capitalize on key moments is becoming their trademark this season.

VRAC marches on — and the title race remains firmly in their hands.
