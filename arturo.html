Big signing at Alcobendas

Published: Spanish Rugby News — October 2025

Alcobendas Rugby has secured a major addition for the upcoming División de Honor season, announcing the signing of Argentine fullback Arturo Devesa.

Devesa, who arrives from the renowned Argentine club Ingenieros Industriales (Indus), brings speed, kicking ability, and creativity from the backfield to the Madrid side.

Known for his outstanding work as a zaguero (fullback), he has represented Argentina at youth levels and is widely considered one of the rising talents in South American rugby.

Alcobendas hopes his arrival will strengthen their attacking structure as they rebuild and push to return to the top of Spanish rugby. Fans are already excited to see Devesa in action wearing the traditional garnet jersey.

Welcome to Spain, Arturo Devesa — and welcome to Alcobendas Rugby!
