Spain prepares for the European Championship

Published: Javier Méndez. October 2025

The Spanish national rugby team is entering the final phase of its preparation for the upcoming European Championship. With an impressive series of warm-up matches, the "Leones" have shown significant progress both in attack and defense.

Coach Santiago Santos emphasized the importance of maintaining discipline and consistency: "We have a young team, but with great potential. Our goal is to compete at the highest level and prove that Spanish rugby belongs among the best in Europe."

The squad will face Georgia, Portugal, and Romania in the group stage. The opening match against Georgia is expected to be a real test for Spain’s physical strength and tactical preparation.

Fans across Spain are excited, and the federation expects record-breaking attendance for the home matches in Butarque (Leganés, Madrid) and José Zorrilla (Valladolid). The success of the national team could also inspire growth in domestic competitions, bringing more visibility to rugby across the country.
